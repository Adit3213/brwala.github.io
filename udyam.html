Unlock Government Benefits for your Business

Why Should You Register?

Benefits of Registering

  • ✔ Collateral Free Loans: Access to Mudra & MSME loans.
  • ✔ Subsidy: Up to 50% subsidy on patent & trademark registration.
  • ✔ Electricity Bill: Concession on electricity bills.
  • ✔ Payment Protection: Legal protection against delayed payments.

Risks of NOT Registering

  • ❌ No Govt Support: Cannot apply for any government tenders.
  • ❌ High Interest Rates: Banks charge higher interest.
  • ❌ No Legal Identity: Hard to prove business existence.

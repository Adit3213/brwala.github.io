Is GST Right for You?

Benefits of GST

  • ✔ Sell Online: Mandatory for Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho.
  • ✔ Input Tax Credit: Claim refunds on tax paid.
  • ✔ Inter-state Sales: Sell goods across India.
  • ✔ Business Loans: Banks prefer GST-registered businesses.

Risks of NOT Registering

  • ❌ Seizure of Goods: Goods can be confiscated during transport.
  • ❌ Heavy Penalties: 100% penalty on tax evaded.
  • ❌ Limited Growth: Cannot sell outside your state.
